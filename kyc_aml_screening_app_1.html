PEP Screening
Politically Exposed Person identification and risk assessment
ℹ️ Under FATF Recommendation 12, financial institutions must apply EDD to all PEPs, their family members, and close associates. This applies regardless of whether the PEP is domestic or foreign. The obligation continues for a reasonable period after the person leaves office (typically 12–24 months minimum).

SAR / STR Filing
Suspicious Activity Report · Suspicious Transaction Report (India: FIU-IND)
⚠️ Legal obligation: Under PMLA 2002 Section 12, all reporting entities must file an STR with FIU-IND within 7 working days of establishing grounds of suspicion. Tipping off the subject is a criminal offence under Section 14A. This tool is for internal preparation only — actual filing must be done on the FIU-IND portal.
SAR Preparation Form
PMLA / FIU-IND Reporting Framework
STR — Suspicious Transaction Report ▼
Filed when any transaction is suspected to involve proceeds of crime, money laundering, or terrorism financing. Deadline: 7 working days from suspicion arising. No minimum transaction amount. Filed with FIU-IND via their secure portal.
CTR — Cash Transaction Report ▼
Mandatory filing for all cash transactions above ₹10 lakhs (or equivalent in foreign currency) in a single day, or series of connected transactions. Monthly filing with FIU-IND. Covers cash deposits, withdrawals, exchanges.
CCR — Counterfeit Currency Report ▼
Filed when counterfeit currency notes are tendered or discovered. Must be reported to FIU-IND and also to the local police / RBI as appropriate. Immediate filing obligation.
CBWTR — Cross Border Wire Transfer Report ▼
All cross-border wire transfers of ₹5 lakhs or more (or equivalent) must be reported to FIU-IND on a monthly basis. Covers both incoming and outgoing wire transfers.
NPM — Non-Profit Organisation Report ▼
Reports related to transactions involving non-profit organisations. Given FATF's focus on NPO sector abuse for terrorism financing (Recommendation 8), enhanced monitoring applies to transactions above ₹10 lakhs.
